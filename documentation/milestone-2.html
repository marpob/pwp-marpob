Content Strategy for Personal Website (Milestone 2)

My site will be three pages, and will consist of the following sections:

Welcome

Welcome (Home page): A brief two lines introduction that clearly states my name and role as a Full Stack Web Developer and Tennis Addict. This welcome message will be overlaid on a large background image of tennis court and computer.

Welcome (Tennis page): A brief two lines introduction that clearly states my name and role as a Tennis Coach. This welcome message will be overlaid on a large background image of a tennis court and me.

Welcome (Full Stack Web Developer)A brief two lines introduction that clearly states my name and role as a Full Stack Web Developer. This welcome message will be overlaid on a large background image of a office and computer.

About me

About Me (Home page): This section will detail my professional history in coaching tennis and in the web development, core skill set, and professional highlights. This section will showcase my strengths and personality. On the bottom of this section I will list my core skills in tennis and in web development which will be easily readable. The tone of voice will be personable and casual, as I am only interested in opportunities with individuals and organizations that I am a good personality fit for.

About Me (Tennis page): This section will detail my professional history in coaching tennis, core skill set, and professional highlights. This section will showcase my strengths and personality. On the bottom of this section I will list my core skills in tennis which will be easily readable. The tone of voice will be personable and casual, as I am only interested in opportunities with individuals and organizations that I am a good personality fit for.

About Me (Full Stack Web Developer): This section will detail my professional history in Full Stack Web Development, core skills set, and professional highlights. This section will showcase my strengths and personality. On the bottom of this section I will list my core skills in Full Stack Web Development which will be easily readable. The tone of voice will be personable and casual, as I am only interested in opportunities with individuals and organizations that I am a good personality fit for.

Portfolio (Home page): This will be animated, responsive image gallery with headings containing 6 and more examples of my best work as a Full Stack Developer and achievements as a tennis coach/player. Gallery will include my role and contributions to each project, with iconography to represent the technology used.

Portfolio (Tennis page): This will be animated, responsive image gallery with headings containing 6 and more examples of my best achievements as a tennis coach and tennis player. Gallery will include my skills and learning curve, with iconography to highlight my skills.

Portfolio (Full Stack Web Developer page): This will be animated, responsive image gallery with heading containing up to 6 examples of my best work as a Full Stack Web Developer. Gallery will include my role and contributions to each project, with iconography to represent the technology used.

Contact (Home page, Tennis page, Full Stack Web Developer page): This section will contain a simple contact form, my professional email address, and additional links to my professional social media profiles: LinkedIn and GitHub (These will also be linked to in the footer site via icons).
